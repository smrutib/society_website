    About Us

    We are here because we serve better.This attitude is the driving force to constantly Improve on our services and Add Values to our services. Our reputation and success lies in our services. “Once a Member, always a Member.” Valentine Apts., established in 2016,was eveloped by Veena Developers.It is a MHADA society with 4 wings ,1 Garden and 1 swimming pool.

    Onging Projects

    Rain water harvesting The society in 2016 installed three recharge structures to harvest rainwater from the terrace, ground, as well as the water that flows down a nearby hill. The water thus collected is channelled into a 105ft deep filtration pit.

    Our Future Plans

    We are set to cut back electricity expenses by 65%, we will inaugurated our 180- kilowatt power (kWp) solar plant by 2020. It will the largest such project in the suburbs. With 546 panels, the project will power lights in the common area, 12 lifts and five water pumps for 622 flats in the society.

    Our Vision

    To develop cooperative and mutual environment amongst the members of the housing society
